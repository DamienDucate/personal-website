about the blog

What Vite Spot was, and what the Vite ecosystem meant

Vite Spot was an independent WordPress blog dedicated to the Vite blockchain — a DAG-based layer-1 focused on instant, feeless transactions. I wrote guides, tracked ecosystem projects, and published Vitely, a weekly news recap for the community.

Every featured image was designed by hand in Affinity Designer on Mac, often taking hours per graphic. The site covered ViteX, VITCSwap, Vitamin Coin (VITC), Viterium, DAO governance, NFTs, and the wider DeFi tooling built on Vite.

In total, the site published 14 articles about Vite and the ecosystem, plus 8 Vitely weekly recaps — 22 posts across guides, tutorials, and news.

This page is a memorial archive — not a live blog. Original posts live in the Wayback Machine. The graphics and structure here preserve what the site was at its peak.

  • Guides 14 in-depth articles on Vite, ViteX, DEXs, DeFi, DAOs, and ecosystem tools.
  • Vitely Weekly newsletter recapping DAO votes, DEX launches, and ecosystem drama.
  • Graphics Custom Affinity Designer artwork for every major post and guide.
